PT. ANEKA JARINGAN INDONESIA

INTRODUCTION

PT. Aneka Jaringan Indonesia (AJI) was founded by experienced Indonesian and Malaysian civil engineers and foundation construction practitioners, in affiliation with Aneka Jaringan Sdn Bhd, a well-known foundation constructor in Malaysian Peninsula. Officially incorporated in March 2014, AJI is a contractor specializing in deep foundation whose major expertise are in bored pile and diaphragm wall. As of today, merely after nine years since it started its operation in the early of year 2015, AJI has successfully completed more than one hundred-twenty (120) contracts of foundation construction for various building and infrastructure projects through which it has built its reputation for good workmanship, safety, quality, and timely delivery.
