ABOUT US

PT. ANEKA JARINGAN INDONESIA

INTRODUCTION

PT. Aneka Jaringan Indonesia (AJI) was founded by experienced Indonesian and Malaysian civil engineers and foundation construction practitioners, in affiliation with Aneka Jaringan Sdn Bhd, a well-known foundation constructor in Malaysian Peninsula.

Officially incorporated in March 2014, AJI is a contractor specializing in deep foundation whose major expertise are in bored pile and diaphragm wall. As of today, merely after nine years since it started its operation in the early of year 2015, AJI has successfully completed more than one hundred-twenty (120) contracts of foundation construction for various building and infrastructure projects through which it has built its reputation for good workmanship, safety, quality, and timely delivery.

MISSION

Our mission is to deliver high-quality foundation construction projects that meet client expectations while remaining within competitive budgets. We focus on leveraging innovative technologies, efficient construction practices, and expert knowledge to provide optimal solutions for every project. By fostering strong partnerships and adhering to both local and international standards, we strive to offer exceptional value, build long-term relationships, and lead the industry in reliability, quality, and sustainable growth.

OUR HISTORY

2014

Aneka was established in March 2014.

2015

Aneka began operations, with its first project located in Menteng, Jakarta.

2017

Aneka made its first entry into Sumatra for the Trans Sumatra Toll Road project, specifically in Lampung Province. In the same year, in August, Aneka also undertook its first geothermal project located in North Sumatra.

2023

In February, Aneka expanded to eastern Indonesia, marking its first operation on Ambon Island.

Now

Aneka continues to grow, providing the best service for all our clients, including BUMN/BUMD and private companies.
