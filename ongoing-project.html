ONGOING PROJECTS

HBR2 Zone 3 Toll Road Construction Project
North Jakarta

The Harbour Road (HBR) II project involves the construction of an elevated toll road connecting Ancol Timur to Pluit in North Jakarta and is part of the National Strategic Project (PSN). This toll road will link the Tanjung Priok Access Toll Road with the Ir. Sedyatmo Toll Road, improving traffic flow and enhancing the competitiveness of Tanjung Priok Port. The project aims to reduce congestion and expedite traffic movement from the east to the north and west. PT. Aneka Jaringan Indonesia serves as the executing contractor under PT. Girder Indonesia, responsible for the foundation work of this significant infrastructure project. This collaboration underscores PT. Aneka Jaringan Indonesia's expertise in delivering critical infrastructure that supports national development goals.

Serang-Panimbang Main Toll Road Project Package 1
Legok, Banten

The Serang-Panimbang Toll Road project is a Public-Private Partnership (PPP) initiative located in Banten Province. This toll road aims to improve access to Ujung Kulon National Park and the Tanjung Lesung Special Economic Zone (KEK). PT. Aneka Jaringan Indonesia plays an active role in the construction of the foundation, ensuring the smooth progress and stability of this vital toll road infrastructure. Through its expertise, PT. Aneka Jaringan Indonesia contributes significantly to enhancing connectivity and supporting regional economic development in Banten.

Bocimi Section 3A Project (Campedak Bridge)
Sukabumi, West Java

The construction of Section 3 of the Bogor, Ciawi-Sukabumi (Bocimi) Toll Road, spanning 13.7 kilometers from West Sukabumi to Cibadak, is progressing steadily. This toll road aims to enhance connectivity and support economic growth in the southern region of West Java, including Sukabumi, Ciawi, and Bogor. PT. Aneka Jaringan Indonesia is actively involved in the foundation construction, ensuring the stability and smooth development of this toll road infrastructure. The project is being carried out under the supervision of PT. Waskita Karya (Persero), Tbk., as the main contractor, with PT. Aneka Jaringan Indonesia contributing its expertise to support the successful completion of this critical infrastructure initiative.

Hydrogen Peroxide Plant Project
Serang, Banten

The hydrogen peroxide plant, which produces a crucial chemical compound for various industries including hydroponics, plays a strategic role in supporting modern agriculture and other sectors in Indonesia. For the construction of this plant in Cilegon, PT. EPTCO Dian Persada, as the main contractor, has partnered with PT. Aneka Jaringan Indonesia for the construction project. This collaboration highlights PT. Aneka Jaringan Indonesia's expertise in contributing to the development of essential industrial infrastructure, further strengthening its reputation as a reliable partner in large-scale and impactful projects.

Riverie Bridge Project at Shilla Sawangan
Sawangan, Depok

The Riverie Bridge project in the Shilla at Sawangan residential area, located in Sawangan, Depok, is being constructed to enhance mobility for the residents of the community. Ancol Timur-Pluit Elevated Toll Road Project
North Jakarta
