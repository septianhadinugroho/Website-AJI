Latest Project: Harbour Road II Toll Road

The Harbour Road II Toll Road project has officially begun, with PT. Wijaya Karya (Persero), Tbk as the main contractor. This project is strategically located near Jakarta International Stadium and adjacent to other ongoing projects by PT. Aneka Jaringan Indonesia. The Harbour Road II Toll Road is expected to enhance connectivity in the surrounding area, provide better access for the community, and support regional economic growth. With the progress of this project, PT. Aneka Jaringan Indonesia is also committed to supporting infrastructure development around Jakarta and actively participating in projects that can improve the quality of life for the community.

Inauguration of the New Pool at PT. Aneka Jaringan Indonesia

PT. Aneka Jaringan Indonesia proudly announces the inauguration of its new pool located at the company’s head office complex. The inauguration event, held on September 19, 2024, was attended by the management team and employees. The purpose of the new pool is to enhance the storage capacity for construction equipment and materials, as well as to support the operational efficiency of the company. The inauguration ceremony was marked by the traditional cutting of the cone (tumpeng) by Mr. Tung Sin Thian and Mr. Ngoi Tong King, directors of PT. Aneka Jaringan Indonesia. The atmosphere was filled with enthusiasm among the attendees, reflecting the great hopes for improved company performance. PT. Aneka Jaringan Indonesia remains committed to enhancing the quality of its services and operations. This new pool represents a strategic step toward achieving the company’s vision as a leader in the construction industry.
